## üß≠ Repository Description

**Tarot Reflection App** is a Tarot-based application designed for **psychological reflection, creative exploration, and positive motivation**.
Tarot is treated as a **symbolic storytelling system**, not a tool for fortune-telling or superstition.

The app helps users slow down, reflect on their inner state, and gain gentle encouragement through meaningful symbols, thoughtful prompts, and small actionable suggestions.

---

## üå± Core Philosophy

* Tarot **does not predict the future**
* Tarot is a **language of symbols and reflection**
* Every interpretation is:

  * Psychologically grounded
  * Non-deterministic and non-absolute
  * Oriented toward clarity, autonomy, and emotional well-being

> ‚ö†Ô∏è This application is for entertainment and positive self-reflection only. It does not replace professional, medical, or psychological advice.

## ‚ú® Key Features

### 1. Context-Based Tarot Drawing

Users can choose:

* **Themes**

  * Love & relationships
  * Work & study
  * Personal growth
  * Current emotional state
* **Reading depth**

  * Quick draw (1 card)
  * Reflection spread (3 cards)
  * Journey spread (5 cards)

---

### 2. Positive & Reflective Card Interpretation

Each Tarot card is interpreted through four layers:

1. **Symbolic Meaning**
   Core imagery, colors, and symbolic themes

2. **Psychological Reflection**
   Possible inner states, emotions, or behavioral patterns

3. **Positive Guidance**
   Small, realistic actions or mindset shifts

4. **Closing Encouragement**
   A gentle reminder that choice and responsibility remain with the user

No predictions. No fear-based language. No absolute claims.

---

### 3. Tarot as a Personal Reflection Journal

* Save past readings
* Write personal notes and feelings
* Observe emotional patterns over time

---

### 4. Daily Card Message

* One card per day
* A short inspirational message to start the day

---

### 5. Tarot Learning Mode

* Explore all 78 cards (Major & Minor Arcana)
* Learn symbolic meanings and psychological themes
* No card drawing required

---

## üß† Design Approach

* Tarot is modeled as an **abstract symbolic system**, not a fixed rulebook
* The same card can appear multiple times with:

  * Different wording
  * Different emphasis
  * Consistent emotional tone
* This avoids repetition, contradiction, and emotional dependency
